# Virtual Reality Sickness Questionnaire (VRSQ)

The standard Virtual Reality Sickness Questionnaire (VRSQ) was administered at the end of the study [[1]](#1). The VRSQ asks participants to rate their state across the following dimensions:

* General Discomfort
* Fatigue
* Headache
* Eye Strain
* Difficulty Focusing
* Fullness of the Head
* Blurred Vision
* Dizziness with Eyes Closed
* Vertigo

| Dataset Column | Question | Response Value | 
| ------------- | ------------- | ------------- |
| 1 | Participant ID | Unique 5-character ID assigned to each participant |
| 2 | General Discomfort | None; Slight; Moderate; Severe |
| 3 | Fatigue | None; Slight; Moderate; Severe |
| 4 | Headache | None; Slight; Moderate; Severe |
| 5 | Eye Strain | None; Slight; Moderate; Severe |
| 6 | Difficulty Focusing | None; Slight; Moderate; Severe |
| 7 | Fullness of the Head | None; Slight; Moderate; Severe |
| 8 | Blurred Vision | None; Slight; Moderate; Severe |
| 9 | Dizziness with Eyes Closed | None; Slight; Moderate; Severe |
| 10 | Vertigo | None; Slight; Moderate; Severe |
